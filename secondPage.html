Instructions:
Flip is a timed card memory game. Click the window cards to see what animal pictures they uncover and try to find the matching pictures (Easy Level: 1 minutes, Medium Level: 1:15 minutes & Hard Level: 2 minutes) underneath the cards. Uncover two matching pictures at once to fix them from the game. Eliminate all cards as fast as you can to win the Jungle Flip. Have fun Jungle Flip!
